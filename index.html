Skill 1:
soul steal
 Ability: Damage, BUFF
Aldous unleashes his inner energy to strengthen his next Basic Attack, dealing 200(+100%Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage. If he eliminates an enemy hero/non-hero unit with his attack, Aldous will gain 10/2 stacks of "Soul Steal", respectively. Each stack increases the damage of this skill by 6 permanently. When attacking Minions or Creeps, the damage of strengthened Basic Attack is increases by 300%. Aldous gets 2 extra stack(s) from "Soul Steal" when enemy non-hero units die in a certain range.

Explosion		 Ability: BUFF, CC

He then deals 100(+20%Total Physical Attack)–200(+40%Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to nearby enemies and stuns them for 0.5-1s (scales with defensive stance duration).
Skill 3:
Chase Fate		 Ability: Blink,Damage

When Aldous hits that enemy hero, he will deal [400–750 (+target's 10% Total HP) Physical Damage] and knock the enemy back for 1 second. (Aldous can interrupt target's Recall.png Recall and Arrival.png Arrival as he charges.) (Use this skill once again to cancel the dash, and will blink to the designated direction instead.)
